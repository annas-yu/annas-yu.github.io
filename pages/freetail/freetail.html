HOME WORK ABOUT
I joined the Freetail Family in the second semester of my freshman year. Freetail Hackers is the largest student-run hackathon group in Texas, organizing and managing multiple events per year.

I work among a smaller team of other designers in order to create assets and design all the creative elements that go into running a hackathon. This includes illustrations that are utilized towards(but not limited to) event website design/layouts, promotional graphics and videos on social media platforms, and swag for our participants like t-shirts, stickers, and pins.

Our biggest event of the year is HackTX, which hosts over 700 participants a year.

I work among a group of people who constantly grow and learn together, all while running these events that are creating a conscious impact on our participants. Though most of my personal work is within the creative team, the communication with my friends at corporate, logistics, outreach, and tech has taught me a great deal outside of design.

You can check out our website at freetailhackers.com

Learn-a-thon 2021
Our Learn-a-thon event was our spring 2021 events targeted to beginner coders and people of all backgrounds who were interested in learning about technology. Over this 2-day virtual event, we hosted multiple beginner-friendly workshops about multiple skills and programs that allowed our participants to participate in a hackathon on the second day. We had over 100 participants at this event, who had access to a board of mentors as they created!

You can check out the website at freetailhackers.com/learnathon
